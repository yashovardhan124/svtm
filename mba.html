WELCOME

The Department of Management Studies at Sir Vishveshwaraiah Institute of Science and Technology, Angallu started its service in the year 2010. It is the first Post Graduate Course in the campus, offering the two years Master of Business Administration, affiliated to JNTU, Ananthapur.
This programme was initially started with an intake of 60 students. Based on the industrial demands, the course enlarged to 120 in 2011 and to 240 from the academic year 2014-15.
Recognising that 'change' is the 'mantra' of today's corporate world and the department being efficient in its functioning and curriculum planning, it keeps updating itself to adapt to the ever-changing demands of today's corporate world.
